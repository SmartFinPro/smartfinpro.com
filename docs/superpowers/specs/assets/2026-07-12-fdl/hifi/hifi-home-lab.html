Methodology

We solve for the largest purchase price where both ratios hold at the stress-tested rate: housing costs within 32% of gross income (GDS) and all debt within 44% (TDS). CMHC insurance is added for down payments under 20%.

Worked example

C$145,000 income, C$450/mo debts, C$90,000 down, 25 years at 4.6%: qualifying at 6.6% caps the price at ~C$565k midpoint, with a comfortable range of C$520k–C$610k.

Frequently asked questions

Why is the stress test my ceiling?

Lenders must qualify you at contract +2pp. Your real payment is lower — but approval is decided at the stressed rate.

Is the range guaranteed?

No — lender policies differ. The range shows where approval is likely, not promised.
