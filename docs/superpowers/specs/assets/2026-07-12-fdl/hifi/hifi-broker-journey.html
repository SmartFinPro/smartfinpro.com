How matching works

Each provider is scored against your experience, instruments, trading frequency, trade size and stated priority. Cost estimates apply published fee schedules to your pattern. Providers without verified availability in your market are excluded entirely.

Frequently asked questions

Do brokers pay for a better match score?

No. Scores come from the published methodology; commercial relationships never change a score, and every recommendation shows its reasoning.

What happens to my answers?

They stay in your browser session and travel with you through the journey. Closing the tab clears them.
