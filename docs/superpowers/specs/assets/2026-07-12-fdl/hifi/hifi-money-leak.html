How this scanner works

We compare each spending category against benchmarks for your income band and household size. Anything meaningfully above the benchmark counts toward your leak estimate — shown as a range, because real households vary.

Worked example

A two-person household with $5,400 net income, $80 subscriptions, $320 insurance and $24 banking fees sees an estimated leak of $210–$340/month — driven mostly by an insurance premium ~40% above the benchmark.

Frequently asked questions

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. Your inputs stay in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server unless you request the email report.

Why a range instead of one number?

Because benchmarks are averages. A range is honest; a single number would be false precision.
