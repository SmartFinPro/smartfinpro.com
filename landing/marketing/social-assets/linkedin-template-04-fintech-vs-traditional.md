```
Unpopular opinion in finance:

Traditional banks are NOT always worse than fintechs for business banking.

Here's when the old-school option actually wins:

---

Cash-heavy businesses
→ You need branch access
→ Fintechs can't help you deposit $10K in twenties

Credit needs
→ Business line of credit?
→ Relationship banking still matters

International complexity
→ Multi-entity structures
→ Trade finance, letters of credit

Specific integrations
→ Some ERPs play nicer with traditional banks

Fintechs win on:
• Fees (often $0 vs $15-30/mo)
```

```
The business banking question nobody asks:

Most founders choose banks based on:

❌ Signup bonuses

✅ How many transactions per month?
✅ Do you handle physical cash?
✅ International payments?
```
