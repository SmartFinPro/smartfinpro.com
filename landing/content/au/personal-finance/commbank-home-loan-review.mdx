### Loan Types Available

CommBank offers a comprehensive range of home loan products to suit different borrower needs:

|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Standard Variable** | Variable | Basic variable loan with redraw |

| **Green Home Loan** | Variable | Discounted rate for energy-efficient homes |

### Wealth Package Features

The Wealth Package (A$395 per year) unlocks CommBank's most competitive rates and features:

- **Discounted variable rate** — typically 0.10-0.15% below the standard variable rate
- **100% offset account** — reduce interest by parking savings against your loan
- **Fee-free Platinum credit card** — annual fee waived (saving up to A$249)
- **Insurance discounts** — up to 10% off CommInsure home and contents insurance
- **No loan establishment fee** — saving A$600 on setup costs
- **Unlimited additional repayments** on variable loans with free redraw

<Tip>
**Offset account strategy:** If you have A$50,000 or more in savings, a 100% offset account on a Wealth Package loan can save you significantly more than the A$395 annual fee. On a A$600,000 loan at 6.34%, A$50,000 in offset saves approximately A$3,170 per year in interest.
</Tip>

### Regulatory Compliance Verification

We verified CommBank's regulatory standing as part of our review:

- **APRA registration:** Confirmed as Authorised Deposit-taking Institution
- **ASIC licensing:** Australian Financial Services Licence verified on ASIC Connect
- **Financial Claims Scheme:** Confirmed deposit protection up to A$250,000 per account holder
- **AFCA membership:** Confirmed membership for external dispute resolution
- **National Credit Code:** Confirmed compliance with responsible lending obligations under the National Consumer Credit Protection Act 2009

<Info>
**APRA vs ASIC regulation:** CommBank is regulated by both APRA (prudential supervision as an ADI) and ASIC (conduct and licensing). APRA ensures CommBank holds sufficient capital reserves and manages risk prudently. ASIC ensures CommBank treats borrowers fairly and complies with the National Credit Code. This dual regulation provides comprehensive protection for home loan borrowers.
</Info>
