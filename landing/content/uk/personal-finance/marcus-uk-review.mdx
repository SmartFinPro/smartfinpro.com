## Eligibility Requirements

### Basic Requirements

To apply for a Marcus personal loan, you must meet the following criteria:

- **Age:** 18 years or older
- **Residency:** UK resident
- **Income:** Regular income sufficient to afford repayments
- **Bank account:** UK bank account for repayments via Direct Debit
- **Identification:** Valid UK photo ID (passport or driving licence)
- **Credit history:** No bankruptcies, IVAs, or CCJs

### Credit Score Guidance

Marcus uses data from multiple credit reference agencies to assess your application:

| Credit Agency | Score Range | Minimum for Approval | Score for Best Rate (6.7%) |
|---------------|------------|----------------------|---------------------------|
| Experian | 0-999 | ~800+ | 961+ |
| Equifax | 0-700 | ~420+ | 531+ |
| TransUnion | 0-710 | ~604+ | 628+ |

<Warning>
**Note on credit requirements:** Marcus's best-in-market 6.7% APR is typically reserved for borrowers with excellent credit scores. If your credit profile is less than excellent, your personalised rate may be significantly higher. Always check your eligibility via the soft search tool before formally applying.
</Warning>

### What Marcus Looks For

Beyond credit scores, Marcus assesses:

- **Income stability:** Consistent employment or verifiable self-employment income
- **Debt-to-income ratio:** Your existing monthly debt payments relative to your income
- **Credit utilisation:** How much of your available credit you are currently using
- **Payment history:** Whether you have missed payments on existing credit agreements
- **Residential stability:** How long you have lived at your current address

## Application Process

### Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1: Soft Search Eligibility Check (3-5 minutes)**
Visit the Marcus website and enter your details to see your personalised rate. This uses a soft credit search visible only to you.

**Step 2: Review Your Personalised Quote**
Marcus displays your exact APR, monthly repayment amount, and total cost of borrowing. You can adjust the loan amount (£3,500-£40,000) and term (3-6 years) to see different options.

**Step 3: Formal Application (10-15 minutes)**
If you proceed, you will need to provide:
- Full legal name and date of birth
- Current and previous addresses (3 years)
- Employment details and annual income
- Monthly expenditure information
- Purpose of the loan
- Details of existing debts (for consolidation loans)

**Step 4: Decision & Agreement**
Marcus provides a decision within minutes for most applications. If approved, your loan agreement is provided electronically for review and digital signature.

**Step 5: Funding**
Funds are typically transferred within 1-3 working days. For debt consolidation loans with direct creditor payments, Marcus sends funds directly to your nominated creditors.

## Debt Consolidation with Marcus

Marcus is our top recommendation for debt consolidation loans in the UK, thanks to the lowest representative APR and the ability to pay creditors directly on your behalf.

### Direct Creditor Payments

Unlike most lenders who simply transfer loan funds to your bank account, Marcus offers direct creditor payments:

1. **You provide creditor details** during your application
2. **Marcus pays your creditors** directly from the loan proceeds
3. **Any remaining balance** is transferred to your bank account
4. **You manage one payment** — your monthly Marcus Direct Debit

This approach ensures your existing debts are cleared immediately and removes the temptation to spend the loan proceeds elsewhere.
