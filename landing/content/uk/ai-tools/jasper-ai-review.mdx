## FCA Compliance Considerations

<Warning>
**Regulatory Notice:** Any content promoting financial products or services in the UK must comply with FCA rules, including COBS 4 (Communicating with clients) and the Consumer Duty. Jasper AI output is a starting point, not compliant final copy.
</Warning>

### How UK Firms Use Jasper Compliantly

**What Jasper CAN Help With:**
- Educational content (market commentary, explainers)
- Email newsletter drafts
- Social media post ideas
- Client communication templates
- Website copy first drafts

**What Still Needs Human Review:**
- Any performance claims or projections
- Product recommendations or comparisons
- Risk warnings and disclosures
- Personalised advice communications

### Our Compliance Workflow

```
1. Jasper creates initial draft (10 minutes)
2. Adviser reviews for accuracy (15 minutes)
3. Compliance reviews for FCA rules (20 minutes)
4. Final publication

Total: 45 minutes vs 2+ hours traditionally
```

<Tip>
**Pro Tip:** Train Jasper's brand voice to automatically include phrases like "capital at risk" and "past performance" where appropriate. This reduces compliance review time significantly.
</Tip>
