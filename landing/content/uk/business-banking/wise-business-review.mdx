## UK Regulation & Fund Protection

### FCA Authorisation

Wise is authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority as an Electronic Money Institution (EMI).

| Regulatory Status | Details |
|-------------------|---------|
| **FCA Registration** | FRN 900507 |
| **Authorisation Type** | Electronic Money Institution |
| **Safeguarding** | 100% of customer funds in segregated accounts |
| **Partner Banks** | Barclays, J.P. Morgan, Wells Fargo |
| **FSCS Protection** | No (e-money, not deposits) |

### How Your Money is Protected

<Warning>
**Important:** Wise is NOT a bank. Your funds are safeguarded but not covered by the FSCS £85,000 guarantee. For businesses holding large GBP balances that need FSCS protection, consider using Starling or Tide alongside Wise.
</Warning>

**Safeguarding explained:**
- Wise must hold 100% of customer funds in segregated accounts
- These funds are ring-fenced from Wise's operational money
- If Wise failed, safeguarded funds would be returned to customers
- Funds are held at major banks including Barclays
