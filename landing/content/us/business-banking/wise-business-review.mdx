## What is Wise Business?

Wise (formerly TransferWise) is a financial technology company that provides international money transfer and multi-currency account services. Founded in 2011 by two Estonian entrepreneurs frustrated with hidden banking fees, Wise now processes over $12 billion monthly for 16 million customers.

- **Founded:** 2011, London
