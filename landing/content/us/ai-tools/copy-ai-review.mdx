## What is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is an AI-powered content platform designed for speed and efficiency. Launched in 2020, it's built for marketers who need to produce large volumes of short-form content quickly.

For finance professionals, Copy.ai excels at:
- **Social media content** for financial advisors and firms
- **Email marketing** campaigns and sequences
- **Ad copy** for paid campaigns
- **Product descriptions** for fintech products
- **Quick blog post drafts** and outlines

## Key Features

### 1. Workflows (Automation)

Copy.ai's Workflows feature automates repetitive content tasks. Create a template once, then generate variations with one click.

**Example workflow for finance:**
1. Input: Client success story
2. Output: LinkedIn post + Twitter thread + Email announcement + Blog outline

In our testing, workflows saved **3-4 hours per week** on content repurposing.

### 2. 90+ Templates

Copy.ai offers templates organized by content type:

| Category | Templates | Finance Use Case |
|----------|-----------|------------------|
| Social Media | 15+ | LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads |
| Email | 12+ | Client newsletters, sequences |
| Ads | 10+ | Google Ads, Facebook campaigns |
| Blog | 8+ | Outlines, introductions |
| Sales | 10+ | Cold outreach, follow-ups |

<Tip>
The "Pain-Agitate-Solution" email template works exceptionally well for financial services marketing. We saw 34% higher open rates using Copy.ai-generated subject lines.
</Tip>

### 3. Infobase (Brand Context)

The Infobase feature lets you store company information that Copy.ai references in all outputs:

- Company description and values
- Product information
- Target audience details
- Tone and style guidelines
- Compliance requirements

<Info>
For finance teams, we recommend adding compliance disclaimers and regulatory language to your Infobase. Copy.ai will incorporate these automatically.
</Info>

### 4. Chat Interface

Beyond templates, Copy.ai offers a chat interface for freeform requests. Ask it to:
- Brainstorm content ideas
- Rewrite existing copy
- Translate content
- Summarize long documents
