### FCA Compliance and AI Content

<Warning>
**Regulatory Notice:** AI-generated content for UK financial services must comply with FCA rules, including COBS 4 and Consumer Duty requirements. AI output should be treated as a first draft requiring compliance review.
</Warning>

**What AI can help with:**
- Educational blog content
- Newsletter drafts
- Social media posts
- Client email templates
- Marketing copy (with review)

**What still needs human oversight:**
- Specific financial advice
- Product recommendations
- Performance claims
- Risk disclosures

### British English and Tone

Most AI tools default to American English. For UK finance content, you'll need to:

1. **Train brand voice** for British spelling (organisation, colour, etc.)
2. **Adjust tone** for professional UK finance audience
3. **Include UK-specific terminology** (ISAs, SIPPs, FCA, etc.)

Jasper AI handles this best with its brand voice feature, whilst ChatGPT requires manual prompting.
