### What FCA Regulation Means for You

As an FCA-authorised broker, IG must comply with strict operational requirements that directly protect your interests as a UK retail client. Your funds are held in segregated accounts at tier-one banks completely separate from IG's corporate capital, meaning your money is ring-fenced even in the unlikely event of IG experiencing financial difficulty. IG must also conduct appropriate assessments to determine whether complex products like CFDs are suitable for your level of experience, and all marketing materials are subject to FCA review for fairness and clarity.

## Account Types

IG offers five distinct account types, each designed for a specific investment or trading objective. You can hold multiple account types simultaneously under a single IG login, which simplifies portfolio management across different strategies and tax wrappers.

### Share Dealing Account

The share dealing account is for buying and holding real shares. You own the underlying equities, receive dividend payments, and hold voting rights. There is no leverage and no overnight financing charges. This is the appropriate account for long-term equity investing and building a stock portfolio.

### CFD Account

The CFD account enables leveraged trading across all of IG's markets. You can go long or short, trade on margin, and access instruments that are not available for physical delivery. Overnight financing charges apply to positions held past market close. Retail clients receive negative balance protection and leverage limits set by the FCA.

### Spread Betting Account

Spread betting is IG's tax-efficient trading vehicle for UK and Ireland residents. Profits are currently free from Capital Gains Tax and Stamp Duty. Like CFDs, spread betting involves leverage and carries significant risk. Positions are quoted in pounds per point of movement rather than in contract sizes.

### Stocks and Shares ISA

IG's ISA wrapper lets you invest up to £20,000 per tax year in shares and ETFs completely free from Capital Gains Tax and dividend tax. There is no annual ISA fee, and the same dealing fees apply as the standard share dealing account. You can hold UK and international shares, ETFs, and investment trusts within the ISA.

### Self-Invested Personal Pension (SIPP)

IG's SIPP provides a tax-efficient pension wrapper with access to the same investment universe as the ISA. The annual fee is 0.25% of portfolio value, capped at £250 per year. You receive tax relief on contributions up to your annual allowance.
