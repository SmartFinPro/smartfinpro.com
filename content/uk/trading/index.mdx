### Why FCA Regulation Matters

All platforms in our top 5 are authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority:

| Protection | Coverage |
|------------|----------|
| **FSCS** | Up to £85,000 per firm |
| **Client money rules** | Funds held separately |
| **Best execution** | Must get you the best price |
| **Clear pricing** | No hidden fees allowed |

### FSCS Protection Explained

The Financial Services Compensation Scheme protects your investments:

- **Coverage:** Up to £85,000 per person, per firm
- **Applies to:** Platform failure, fraud, negligence
- **Does NOT cover:** Investment losses from market movements

<Warning>
**Important:** FSCS protection is per firm, not per account. If you have multiple accounts with the same provider, total protection is still £85,000.
</Warning>

### Verifying FCA Registration

Always verify your broker is FCA registered:
1. Visit the FCA Register: register.fca.org.uk
2. Search by firm name or number
3. Check "Current Status: Authorised"
4. Verify permitted activities include investment dealing
