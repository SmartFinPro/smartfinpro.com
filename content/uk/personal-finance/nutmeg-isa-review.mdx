## ISA Allowance & Tax Benefits

### Understanding Your £20,000 ISA Allowance

Every UK adult (18+) can invest up to **£20,000 per tax year** (6 April to 5 April) in ISAs. With Nutmeg, all investment growth, dividends, and interest within your ISA are completely free from UK income tax and capital gains tax.

### The Four ISA Types

| ISA Type | Description | Annual Limit |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Cash ISA** | Savings with tax-free interest | Up to £20,000 |
| **Stocks and Shares ISA** | Investments (e.g., Nutmeg) | Up to £20,000 |
| **Lifetime ISA** | First home or retirement (18-39) | Up to £4,000 (within the £20,000) |
| **Innovative Finance ISA** | Peer-to-peer lending | Up to £20,000 |

**Remember:** The £20,000 is shared across all ISA types. Nutmeg offers both a Stocks and Shares ISA and a Lifetime ISA.

### Why Managed Investing Beats Cash for Long-Term Goals

For goals 5+ years away, professionally managed portfolios have historically delivered significantly higher returns than cash:

## Portfolio Options & Investment Styles

### Three Management Styles

Nutmeg offers three distinct portfolio management approaches, each at a different fee level:

**1. Fixed Allocation (0.25% management fee)**
- Static portfolio based on your risk level
- Automatically rebalanced to maintain target allocation
- Lowest cost option — best for long-term, set-and-forget investors
- No active management or tactical adjustments

**2. Smart Alpha (0.35% management fee)**
- Powered by J.P. Morgan Asset Management research
- Uses J.P. Morgan's capital market assumptions to optimise returns
- Tactical tilts based on market outlook
- Best balance of cost and active insight

**3. Fully Managed (0.75% management fee)**
- Active portfolio management by Nutmeg's investment team
- Ongoing tactical adjustments based on market conditions
- Most responsive to economic changes
- Highest cost — best for investors who want maximum professional oversight

### Risk Levels (1-10)

Each management style is available across 10 risk levels:

| Risk Level | Equity Allocation | Typical Investor |
|-----------|-------------------|------------------|
| 1-2 | 10-25% equities | Cautious — preserving capital |
| 3-4 | 30-45% equities | Moderate — balanced growth |
| 5-6 | 50-65% equities | Growth — medium-term goals |
| 7-8 | 70-85% equities | Ambitious — long-term growth |
| 9-10 | 90-100% equities | Adventurous — maximum growth |

### Socially Responsible Portfolios

Nutmeg offers a **Socially Responsible Investing (SRI)** option across all risk levels, focusing on:

- **Environmental:** Companies reducing carbon emissions and environmental impact
- **Social:** Companies with strong labour practices and community engagement
- **Governance:** Companies with transparent management and ethical business practices
- **Exclusions:** Tobacco, controversial weapons, thermal coal, and companies violating UN Global Compact principles

<Tip>
**Which style should you choose?** For most ISA investors with a 10+ year horizon, the Fixed Allocation portfolio at 0.25% offers the best value. Research consistently shows that keeping costs low is one of the most reliable ways to improve long-term investment returns. The Smart Alpha option at 0.35% is a reasonable step up if you want the benefit of J.P. Morgan's market insights.
</Tip>

## Platform Experience & Account Setup

### Getting Started (Under 10 Minutes)

Nutmeg's onboarding process is one of the simplest in the UK:

1. **Answer the questionnaire** — Nutmeg asks about your investment goals, time horizon, income, and attitude to risk
2. **Review your portfolio recommendation** — Based on your answers, Nutmeg suggests a risk level (1-10) and management style
3. **Choose your management style** — Fixed Allocation, Smart Alpha, or Fully Managed
4. **Select standard or socially responsible** — Apply ESG screening if desired
5. **Fund your account** — Bank transfer, Direct Debit, or ISA transfer
6. **Nutmeg manages everything** — Ongoing portfolio management and rebalancing

### Web Dashboard

The Nutmeg dashboard is clean and purposefully simple:

- **Portfolio value** with clear performance charts over various timeframes
- **Asset allocation breakdown** showing how your money is invested
- **Projected outcomes** with Monte Carlo simulation showing likely return ranges
- **Contribution tracking** and ISA allowance monitoring
- **Goal tracking** to see progress towards your investment target

### Mobile App

The Nutmeg app (iOS and Android) provides:

- Portfolio overview and performance tracking
- One-tap top-ups and withdrawal requests
- Goal progress monitoring
- Push notifications for portfolio updates
- Biometric login (Face ID / fingerprint)
- In-app messaging with Nutmeg's support team

### What You Cannot Do on Nutmeg

Unlike DIY platforms, Nutmeg does not allow:

- Selecting individual funds or shares
- Choosing specific ETFs or investment trusts
- Setting limit orders or stop losses
- Accessing detailed fund research or analyst reports
- Making tactical decisions about your portfolio allocation

**Our take:** This is by design. Nutmeg's entire value proposition is removing the burden of investment decisions. For investors who want control, Hargreaves Lansdown or AJ Bell are better choices.
