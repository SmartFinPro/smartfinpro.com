## ISA Allowance & Tax Benefits

### Your £20,000 Annual ISA Allowance

Every UK resident aged 18 or over can invest up to **£20,000 per tax year** (6 April to 5 April) inside an ISA. All returns within the HL ISA — dividends, interest, and capital gains — are completely tax-free.

### ISA Types Available at Hargreaves Lansdown

HL offers three of the four ISA types, giving you flexibility to use your allowance strategically:

| ISA Type | Available at HL | Annual Limit | Key Feature |
|----------|----------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Stocks and Shares ISA** | Yes | Up to £20,000 | Invest in funds, shares, ETFs |
| **Lifetime ISA** | Yes | Up to £4,000 | 25% government bonus |
| **Cash ISA** | Yes (Active Savings) | Up to £20,000 | Tax-free savings interest |
| **Innovative Finance ISA** | No | N/A | Not available at HL |

### The Tax Savings in Practice

For a higher-rate taxpayer (40%), the ISA tax benefit is substantial:

| Investment Return | Tax Owed (General Account) | Tax Owed (ISA) | Annual Tax Saving |
|-------------------|--------------------------|----------------|-------------------|
| £2,000 dividends | £662.50* | £0 | £662.50 |
| £5,000 capital gain | £1,000** | £0 | £1,000 |
| £1,000 bond interest | £400 | £0 | £400 |

*After £1,000 dividend allowance at 33.75% | **After £3,000 CGT allowance at 20%

<Info>
**Flexible ISA advantage.** HL's ISA is flexible, meaning withdrawals made during the tax year can be replaced without counting towards your £20,000 allowance. This is a valuable feature if you may need temporary access to your funds.

### Client Protection Measures

1. **FSCS coverage:** Up to **£85,000** per person if HL were to fail as a firm
2. **Nominee accounts:** Your investments are held in HL's nominee company, legally separate from HL's own assets
3. **FCA Client Money Rules:** Cash held by HL is ring-fenced in accordance with FCA CASS rules
4. **Annual audit:** HL's client asset arrangements are independently audited annually
5. **Financial Ombudsman Service:** Free complaint escalation if you are dissatisfied with HL's response

<Info>
**FSCS protection covers firm failure, not investment losses.** The £85,000 FSCS protection applies if Hargreaves Lansdown as a company fails. It does not protect against the value of your investments falling due to market movements. The value of investments can go down as well as up.
</Info>

## ISA Transfer & Switching Guide

### Transferring to Hargreaves Lansdown

HL makes ISA transfers straightforward and offers both cash and in-specie (stock) transfers:

**Cash transfer:** Your existing investments are sold, the cash is transferred to HL, and you reinvest. Typically takes 15-30 working days.

**In-specie transfer:** If HL can hold the same investments (e.g., the same fund or shares), your holdings are moved directly without selling. This avoids being out of the market during the transfer. In-specie transfers may take 4-8 weeks.

**Key transfer benefits at HL:**
- No transfer-in fees
- Transfer tracker to monitor progress online
- Dedicated transfer support team
- Ability to transfer partial ISA holdings from some providers
- Current year and previous year ISAs can be transferred separately

### Consolidating Multiple ISAs

Many UK investors have ISAs spread across several providers from previous tax years. HL's platform makes consolidation straightforward:

- Transfer old Cash ISAs into a Stocks and Shares ISA for higher long-term growth potential
- Combine multiple Stocks and Shares ISAs from different providers into one HL account
- View all your ISA investments in a single portfolio dashboard

<Tip>
**In-specie transfers save money.** If you hold funds that are also available on HL (such as Fundsmith Equity or Vanguard index funds), request an in-specie transfer to avoid selling and rebuying. This eliminates the risk of being out of the market during the transfer and avoids any bid-offer spread costs.
</Tip>
