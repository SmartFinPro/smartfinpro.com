The AJ Bell ISA operates within the standard UK ISA framework, giving every adult (18+) the ability to invest up to **£20,000 per tax year** with all returns — dividends, interest, and capital gains — completely free from UK tax.

<Info>

</Info>

2. **Nominee accounts:** Your investments are held in AJ Bell's nominee company, legally separate from company assets

**FSCS covers firm failure, not market losses.** The £85,000 FSCS protection applies if AJ Bell as a company fails and your assets cannot be returned. It does not cover losses from your investments falling in value due to market conditions. The value of investments can go down as well as up.

## ISA Transfer & Tax Year Planning

### Transferring to AJ Bell

AJ Bell accepts ISA transfers from all UK providers with both cash and in-specie options:

**Cash transfer:** Existing investments are sold, cash transferred to AJ Bell, and you reinvest. Takes 15-30 working days.

**In-specie transfer:** Holdings that AJ Bell can accommodate (same funds, shares, or ETFs) are transferred directly without selling. Takes 4-8 weeks but avoids being out of the market.

**Transfer considerations:**
- No transfer-in fees at AJ Bell
- Online transfer tracking available
- Current year and previous year ISA transfers handled separately
- Partial transfers may be available depending on your existing provider

### Tax Year End Planning

The ISA allowance resets on 6 April each year. To maximise your tax-free investing:

- **Use your full £20,000 allowance** before 5 April if possible
- **Set up a regular investment** to ensure consistent contributions throughout the year
- **Consider bed-and-ISA** — sell investments in a general account and rebuy inside your ISA to shelter future gains
- **Split strategically** between ISA types if using a Lifetime ISA alongside your AJ Bell S&S ISA

<Info>
**Bed-and-ISA explained.** If you hold investments in a general (non-ISA) account, you can sell them and immediately rebuy the same investments inside your AJ Bell ISA. This shelters all future growth from tax. Be aware of the capital gains tax implications on the sale — the current CGT annual allowance is £3,000 (2025/26).
</Info>
