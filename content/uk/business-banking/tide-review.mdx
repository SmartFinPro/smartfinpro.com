    answer: >-

      funds are 'safeguarded' rather than FSCS protected. However, funds are

      insolvency.
  - question: Is my money safe with Tide?
    answer: >-
      Tide safeguards customer funds at Barclays Bank. While not FSCS protected,
      safeguarding means your money is held separately from Tide's business
      funds. In practice, the risk is low, but it's worth understanding the
      difference.
