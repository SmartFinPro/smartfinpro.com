## Gold IRA Eligibility and Considerations

Silver Gold Bull sells IRA-eligible bullion but does **not** operate as an IRA custodian. For US investors wanting a Gold IRA, the process involves three parties:

1. **Self-directed IRA custodian** (Equity Trust, GoldStar Trust, Kingdom Trust) — Manages the account and regulatory compliance
2. **Dealer** (Silver Gold Bull) — Supplies the physical metals meeting IRS purity requirements
3. **IRS-approved depository** (Brinks, Delaware Depository, IDS) — Stores the metals in a qualified facility

This three-party structure offers flexibility: you choose the lowest-premium dealer independently of the custodian's preferred dealers. [Goldco](/gold-investing/goldco-review) offers a simpler all-in-one experience but at higher premiums because the integrated service includes custodian fees and mandatory depository partnerships.

For investors comfortable managing multiple provider relationships, purchasing IRA-eligible bullion from Silver Gold Bull through a SDIRA custodian can save $20-$50 per ounce versus Goldco's integrated pricing. The tradeoff is additional administrative complexity.

<Warning>IRS Section 408(m) requires all IRA-held precious metals to be stored at an IRS-approved US depository. Silver Gold Bull's Calgary storage facility does NOT qualify for IRA storage. If purchasing for an IRA, metals must ship directly to your custodian's designated US depository (e.g., Delaware Depository, Brinks). Home storage of IRA metals is prohibited and triggers immediate taxation plus a 10% penalty.</Warning>
