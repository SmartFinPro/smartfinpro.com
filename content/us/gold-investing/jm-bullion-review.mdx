### Price Match Promise in Practice

JM Bullion's Price Match Promise guarantees the lowest advertised prices for identical products from authorized competing dealers within 5 business days of purchase, per the terms published on its website. The policy eliminates the need for exhaustive comparison shopping across multiple dealer websites, though savvy investors should still verify that the competing price is from an authorized dealer with identical product specifications.

The practical value of the Price Match Promise extends beyond individual savings. It signals that JM Bullion is confident in its pricing competitiveness — the company would not offer this guarantee if it were frequently undercut. Our review of published pricing across [JM Bullion](https://www.jmbullion.com/), [APMEX](https://www.apmex.com/), and [SD Bullion](https://www.sdbullion.com/) found that JM Bullion generally offers premiums that are competitive with or lower than these peers on popular bullion products, though exact standing shifts with market conditions and should be verified at time of purchase.

### Gold vs. Silver vs. Platinum Selection

JM Bullion maintains approximately 5,000+ active SKUs across gold, silver, platinum, and palladium products. While this catalog is extensive for most bullion investors, it trails APMEX's inventory of tens of thousands of SKUs — the gap primarily exists in numismatic coins, historic pieces, and collector-grade items. For investors focused on standard bullion products — American Eagles, Canadian Maple Leafs, generic bars, and rounds — JM Bullion's selection is more than sufficient and often offers better pricing on those high-volume items.

The product breakdown roughly divides into gold (1,500+ products), silver (2,500+ products), platinum (500+ products), and palladium (200+ products), with the remainder spanning copper rounds and promotional items. Silver's dominance in the catalog reflects JM Bullion's heritage as a silver-focused dealer. Gold products include US Mint Eagles, Canadian Maple Leafs, South African Krugerrands, Austrian Philharmonics, and generic bars from major refiners including PAMP Suisse, Valcambi, and the Royal Canadian Mint.

### Shipping Speed and Packaging Quality

JM Bullion advertises shipping within 3–7 business days from the order date, a timeframe corroborated by the positive shipping feedback from [Trustpilot reviewers](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/jmbullion.com). Reviewers commonly describe tamper-evident packaging with appropriate cushioning, with coins individually sealed in protective flips or capsules and larger bar orders including additional foam inserts within the shipping box.

Shipping delays are possible during high-volume periods when precious metals markets experience significant price movements. These surges drive increased order volumes across all dealers, not just JM Bullion. Orders placed Monday through Thursday typically ship within 2–3 business days; Friday orders often ship the following Monday or Tuesday.

## How JM Bullion Makes Money

Understanding JM Bullion's revenue model helps investors assess whether the platform's incentives align with their interests. Unlike subscription-based financial platforms that earn recurring fees regardless of customer outcomes, JM Bullion's revenue is fundamentally tied to transaction volume — meaning the company benefits when customers buy and sell actively. This alignment creates a natural incentive to maintain competitive pricing, since overpriced products reduce purchase volume.

1. **Dealer premiums above spot price** — the primary revenue source. Each product is priced at spot + premium, with premiums ranging from +$15 (PAMP Suisse bars) to +$130 (Gold Eagles). Higher-demand products carry higher premiums.

2. **Buyback spread** — when JM Bullion repurchases metals from investors at 1.5–3% below spot, the dealer spread becomes revenue upon resale at spot + premium. Active buying and selling creates a continuous margin cycle.

3. **Payment method optimization** — credit card purchases carry higher premiums than wire/crypto, reflecting processing fee recovery. The differential encourages cost-effective payment methods while generating additional margin on convenience-oriented purchases.

4. **Subscription revenue** — the JMB subscription program generates committed monthly volume at slightly reduced premiums. While per-unit margins are lower, guaranteed recurring volume provides predictable revenue and improved inventory planning.

5. **Volume-based mint and refiner pricing** — JM Bullion's $800+ million annual volume commands bulk pricing from mints and refiners, creating margin advantages that smaller dealers cannot replicate. These savings are partially passed to customers (competitive pricing) and partially retained as profit.
