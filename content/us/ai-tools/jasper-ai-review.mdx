### 2. Brand Voice

The Brand Voice feature is Jasper's strongest differentiator for regulated industries. It allows you to train the AI on your company's tone, style, terminology, and compliance requirements.

**How we configured Brand Voice for a financial advisory firm:**

1. **Uploaded 20 approved content pieces** as style examples
2. **Defined key terms:** "fiduciary," "risk-adjusted returns," "asset allocation"
3. **Set compliance guardrails:** No performance guarantees, always include disclosure language
4. **Specified tone:** Authoritative but approachable, educational not promotional

**Result:** After 30 minutes of initial setup, output quality improved dramatically. Content required 40% fewer compliance edits compared to generic AI tools.

### For SEC/FINRA Regulated Firms

Jasper assists with content creation, but all output must be reviewed by a registered principal before publication. Key compliance considerations:

- **Books and records:** Jasper maintains full content history for SEC Rule 17a-4 documentation
- **Supervision:** Approval workflows support FINRA Rule 3110 supervisory requirements
- **Fair and balanced:** Brand Voice helps maintain fair and balanced communications
- **No guarantees:** Configure guardrails to prevent performance guarantee language
