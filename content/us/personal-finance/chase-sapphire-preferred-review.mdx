## Chase Travel Portal

The Chase Travel portal is where the Sapphire Preferred really shines for casual travelers:

### How It Works

1. **Book through Chase Travel** (flights, hotels, rental cars)
2. **Pay with points** at 1.25 cents each
3. **Or mix** cash + points for any booking
4. **Earn 5x points** on portal bookings (net positive cycle)

### Portal vs. Transfer Partners

| Scenario | Portal Value | Transfer Value |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| 60,000 points | $750 | $600-$1,800 |
| **Ease of use** | **Simple** | Requires research |
| **Flexibility** | Any flight | Partner availability |
| **Best for** | **Most travelers** | Points enthusiasts |

<Tip>
The portal is underrated. For domestic flights and standard hotels, booking through Chase Travel is simpler and guarantees 1.25 cents/point. Save transfer partners for premium international redemptions where value exceeds 1.5 cents/point.
</Tip>

## Our Verdict

After extensive analysis, the Chase Sapphire Preferred earns **4.6 out of 5 stars**.

**Bottom line:** The Chase Sapphire Preferred remains the best value proposition in travel credit cards.
