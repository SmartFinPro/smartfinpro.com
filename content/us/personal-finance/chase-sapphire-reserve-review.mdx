## $300 Travel Credit

The travel credit is the Reserve's primary fee offset:

### What Qualifies

The credit is broader than you might expect:
- Airlines (any airline, any booking method)
- Hotels and vacation rentals
- Car rentals
- Uber, Lyft, taxis
- Tolls and parking
- Public transit
- Travel agencies

### How It Works

1. Use your Reserve for any travel purchase
2. Statement credit automatically applies within 1-2 billing cycles
3. Resets each cardmember year (not calendar year)
4. $300 maximum per year

## Our Verdict

After thorough analysis, the Chase Sapphire Reserve earns **4.5 out of 5 stars**.

**Bottom line:** The Chase Sapphire Reserve is a premium card that delivers premium value—for the right traveler. The $300 travel credit, lounge access, 1.5cpp portal value, and primary rental insurance create genuine value for frequent travelers spending $5,000+ on travel annually. However, for moderate travelers, the Sapphire Preferred at $95 offers 85% of the value at 17% of the fee. Be honest about your travel frequency before committing to $550.

**Final Rating: 4.5/5**
