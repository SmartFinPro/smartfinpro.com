## Tax Optimization: Wealthfront's Biggest Edge

Tax-loss harvesting is where Wealthfront truly separates itself from the competition. The platform scans your portfolio every single day for opportunities to harvest losses, compared to Betterment's less frequent approach.

**How it works:**
1. The system identifies ETFs or stocks that have declined in value
2. It sells those positions to realize the tax loss
3. It immediately purchases a similar (but not identical) asset to maintain your portfolio allocation
4. The harvested losses offset capital gains or up to $3,000 of ordinary income per year

**Our test results:** During our 90-day test with a $75,000 taxable account, Wealthfront's tax-loss harvesting captured an estimated $1,200 in tax losses. Extrapolated to a full year, that translates to approximately $300-$500 in actual tax savings depending on your tax bracket.

For accounts above $100K, direct indexing amplifies these savings substantially by harvesting losses at the individual stock level rather than the ETF level.

## Top Alternatives to Wealthfront

### Betterment — Best for Goal-Based Planning
Betterment matches Wealthfront's 0.25% fee but adds goal-based investing with more flexibility. You can hold multiple goals in separate "buckets" with different allocations. Betterment Premium ($299+/year) adds unlimited CFP access. Choose Betterment if you want human advisor access or more granular goal tracking.

### Schwab Intelligent Portfolios — Best Free Option
Schwab charges no advisory fee at all, making it the cheapest option for cost-conscious investors. The catch: a $5,000 minimum and a mandatory cash allocation (6-30%) that earns below-market interest. Best for investors who already bank with Schwab and prioritize zero fees.

### Fidelity Go — Best for Beginners
Fidelity Go has no minimum deposit and charges 0% for accounts under $25,000. Above $25K, the fee is 0.35%. It is the simplest onboarding experience we tested but lacks tax-loss harvesting entirely. Ideal for beginners starting with small amounts.
