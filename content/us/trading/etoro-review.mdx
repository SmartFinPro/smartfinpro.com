### Real Stocks vs. CFDs

**Important distinction:**
- **Real stocks:** You own the actual share (US stocks for US clients)
- **CFDs:** Contract for difference—no ownership, leveraged trading

eToro defaults to real stocks for unleveraged positions. CFDs are used for:
- Short selling
- Leveraged positions
- Markets where real ownership isn't available
