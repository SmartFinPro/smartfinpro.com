## Copy Trading Deep Dive

Copy trading is eToro's most distinctive feature and the primary reason the platform has attracted 50 million users. The system allows you to allocate capital to experienced traders whose positions are then automatically replicated in your account on a proportional basis. This means a beginner with no market knowledge can gain exposure to professionally managed strategies from day one.

### How Copy Trading Works

The process is straightforward and designed for investors who want market exposure without active management. You browse eToro's Popular Investor program, where top-performing traders apply to share their strategies publicly. Each Popular Investor profile displays a complete track record including annual returns, maximum drawdown, risk score on a 1-10 scale, number of copiers, and total assets under management. Once you find traders whose strategies align with your goals, you allocate a minimum of $200 per trader, and every subsequent trade they execute is mirrored proportionally in your account.

<CollapsibleSection title="Copy Trading Process Steps" count={5}>

1. **Browse Popular Investors:** Filter by performance metrics, risk score, asset class focus, and track record length
2. **Analyze Track Records:** Review detailed statistics including drawdown history, win rate, and portfolio composition
3. **Allocate Funds:** Invest a minimum of $200 per trader with no maximum cap
4. **Automatic Copying:** All trades execute proportionally in your account in real time
5. **Monitor and Adjust:** Stop copying, add funds, or switch traders at any time with no penalty

</CollapsibleSection>

## Platform, Mobile App & Crypto

### Web Platform

eToro's web platform prioritizes accessibility over advanced functionality. The interface is clean and intuitive, with the social feed integrated directly alongside trading screens. One-click copy trading, customizable watchlists, and real-time portfolio tracking make it easy for beginners to navigate without feeling overwhelmed. However, experienced traders will notice the platform's limitations: charting includes approximately 70 technical indicators compared to 300+ on thinkorswim, and there are no advanced order types such as bracket orders or OCO (one-cancels-other) configurations.

### Mobile App & Demo Account

The eToro mobile app is available on iOS and Android and delivers full trading functionality including copy trading management, push notifications for copied trades, and biometric authentication via Touch ID or Face ID. The app has earned a 4.4/5 rating from 245,000 iOS reviews and a 4.3/5 rating from 1.2 million Android reviews, reflecting strong user satisfaction. Mobile-first investors will find the experience comparable to Robinhood in terms of simplicity while offering substantially more features through the social feed and copy trading integration.

eToro's virtual portfolio is among the best demo accounts available from any US broker: $100,000 in virtual funds, mirroring the live platform exactly, never expiring, and resettable to the starting balance at any time. Critically, the demo account includes full access to copy trading, so you can practice selecting Popular Investors and monitoring their performance without risking real capital.

<Info>
Plan on spending at least 30 days on the demo account before committing real capital. Use this period to test your copy trading selections, understand how spreads affect returns, and familiarize yourself with the platform's order execution behavior.
</Info>

### Crypto

eToro provides access to over 80 cryptocurrencies for US investors, making it one of the broader crypto selections among traditional brokerage platforms. Available assets include Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Cardano, Polygon, Avalanche, and dozens of smaller altcoins. Unlike eToro's international entities that offer crypto CFDs, US clients purchase and hold real crypto assets, which can be transferred to the eToro Money crypto wallet for self-custody.

The primary cost consideration is the 1% spread on crypto trades, which is higher than dedicated exchanges like Coinbase Pro (0.5%) or Kraken (0.26%). For investors who value having stocks, ETFs, and crypto in a single account with copy trading across all asset classes, this premium may be justified. For high-volume crypto traders executing frequent positions, a dedicated exchange will deliver better execution economics.

## Our Verdict

eToro occupies a unique position in the US brokerage landscape. No other SEC-registered platform offers a comparable copy trading system, and the combination of zero-commission stock trading, 80+ crypto assets, and an active social community creates genuine value for beginners and passive investors.

**What sets eToro apart:** Copy trading is not a gimmick here — allocating capital across several Popular Investors with different strategies lets beginners observe experienced traders managing positions in real time, which carries real educational value beyond the returns themselves. The social feed adds a genuine layer beyond entertainment too: crowd sentiment on eToro has moved ahead of broader retail platforms during past volatility, though it should be treated as a contrarian indicator alongside fundamentals, not a signal on its own.

**Where it falls short:** eToro is a weaker fit for three investor profiles. Active forex traders face a 1-pip EUR/USD spread roughly 5x wider than [Interactive Brokers' 0.2 pips](/trading/interactive-brokers-review/), which compounds meaningfully at high trade volumes. Advanced technical traders will find the ~70 indicators constraining next to [thinkorswim's 300+](/trading/charles-schwab-review/), with no bracket orders or algorithmic trading. And cost-sensitive investors who withdraw frequently will notice the $5 flat withdrawal fee and $10/month inactivity fee, both of which [Fidelity](/trading/fidelity-review/) and [Robinhood](/trading/robinhood-review/) waive.
