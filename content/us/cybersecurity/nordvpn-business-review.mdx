### Kill Switch

The kill switch prevents data leaks if VPN connection drops:

- **App-level:** Closes specified applications if VPN disconnects
- **System-level:** Blocks all internet traffic until VPN reconnects

In our testing, the kill switch activated within **50ms** of connection loss—fast enough to prevent any data exposure.

### No-Logs Policy

NordVPN maintains a strict no-logs policy:

**What they DON'T log:**
- Browsing history
