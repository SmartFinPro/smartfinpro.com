## Companies to Avoid: Red Flags

### Warning Signs of Credit Repair Scams

❌ **"We'll erase bankruptcies guaranteed"**
- Accurate bankruptcies cannot be removed (10-year reporting)

❌ **Upfront fees before any work**
- Illegal under Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA)

❌ **No physical address or phone number**
- Scam companies hide identities

❌ **"Create new credit identity with EIN"**
- This is fraud (felony offense)

❌ **Pressure to sign immediately**
- Legitimate companies offer 3-day cancellation period (required by law)

### Companies We Do NOT Recommend

**Based on BBB complaints, FTC actions, and consumer reviews:**

1. **CreditRepair.com** — 247 BBB complaints in 2025, F rating
2. **The Credit Pros** — Aggressive sales tactics, hidden fees
3. **Credit Assistance Network** — FTC settlement 2024 for deceptive practices

**Always verify:**
- BBB rating (A+ or A minimum)
- State licensing (required in some states)
- CROA compliance disclosures

## Frequently Asked Questions (Extended)

### Can I use multiple credit repair companies at once?

**No.** Filing duplicate disputes (same errors to same bureaus) can backfire:

- Bureaus may flag as frivolous disputes
- Results become unclear (who gets credit for removals?)
- Wastes money

**Choose one company.** If unsatisfied after 60-90 days, switch.

### What if credit repair doesn't work?

If zero items are removed after 90 days:

1. **Invoke money-back guarantee** (The Credit People, Sky Blue offer 90 days)
2. **Request documentation** of disputes filed
3. **File complaint** with BBB if company didn't perform services
4. **Consider DIY approach** (your errors may be unique)

**Reality:** 85-90% of clients see at least some removals. Complete failure is rare.

### Can credit repair companies guarantee a specific score?

**No.** FTC forbids guarantees of specific score increases. Ethical companies guarantee:

- Effort (filing disputes, interventions)
- Refunds if zero items removed
- Compliance with FCRA

**Beware:** Companies promising "100-point increases guaranteed" are scams.

### Will credit repair hurt my score?

**No.** Disputing errors:

- Does NOT trigger hard inquiries
- Does NOT lower scores
- Can ONLY improve or maintain scores

**Outcome possibilities:**
- Item removed → Score increases
- Item verified → Score unchanged
- No downside risk

### How many items can be disputed at once?

**No legal limit.** However:

- Bureaus may flag 20+ simultaneous disputes as "frivolous"
- Best practice: 10-15 items per round
- File new round every 30-45 days

**Professional services know optimal dispute volumes** to avoid frivolous flags.

### What happens to items removed later re-appear?

**By law (FCRA 611(a)(5)):** Bureaus must notify you before re-inserting removed items.

**Your rights:**
- 5-day advance notice required
- Creditor must provide new evidence
- You can re-dispute with additional proof

**If re-inserted without notice:** File CFPB complaint (FCRA violation).
