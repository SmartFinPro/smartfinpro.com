<ExecutiveSummary title="The Hidden Cost Killing Your International Business">

**The Problem:** You just closed a deal with a client in Germany. They paid €15,000. Your traditional bank charged you $45 for the incoming wire, converted at a 3.5% markup, and took 4 days to credit your account. Total cost: **$570 in hidden fees.** Multiply that by 12 months, and you're bleeding $6,840 annually—money that should be funding your growth.

**The Agitation:** This isn't just about fees. It's about competitive disadvantage. While you're waiting 4 days for funds to clear, your competitor using Wise receives the same payment in hours at 0.5% cost. They're reinvesting that $6,000+ into marketing, hiring, or product development. Every international transaction widens the gap.

**The Solution:** Modern fintech business accounts have eliminated these pain points. We spent 6 months testing 18 business banking options with real money, real transactions, and real international transfers to find the best solutions for US businesses operating globally.

</ExecutiveSummary>
