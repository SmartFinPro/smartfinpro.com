## International Payments: The Honest Truth

Mercury's weakness is international—here's the workaround:

### Mercury's International Options

- **Wire fee:** $5 flat
- **FX markup:** ~1.5% (via partner)
- **Speed:** 2-4 business days

### The Mercury + Wise Strategy

Most Mercury users pair it with Wise for international:

1. **Mercury** for US banking (wires, ACH, payroll)
2. **Wise** for international payments (40+ currencies, 0.5% FX)

**Combined cost:** $0/month (both free accounts)
**Combined benefit:** Best of both worlds

**Pro tip:** Set up automatic transfers from Mercury to Wise when international payments are needed. Many businesses keep a standing Wise balance for frequent vendor payments.
