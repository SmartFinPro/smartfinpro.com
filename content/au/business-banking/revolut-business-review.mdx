## Australian Pricing (AUD)

Revolut offers three tiers for Australian businesses:

| Plan | Monthly Fee | FX Fee | Int'l Transfers | Cards |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------------|-------|
| **Free** | A$0 | 1.0% | 5 free/month | 1 physical |
| **Grow** | A$39 | 0.4% | 50 free/month | 10 cards |
| **Scale** | A$149 | 0% | **Unlimited** | Unlimited |

### Break-Even Analysis: Revolut vs. Wise

| Monthly Volume | Best Choice | Why |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| Under A$20,000 | **Wise** | Pay-per-transaction cheaper |
| A$20,000-100,000 | **Wise or Grow** | Similar costs |
| A$100,000-200,000 | **Revolut Grow** | 50 free transfers |
| Over A$200,000 | **Revolut Scale** | Unlimited free transfers |

<Tip>
**Decision Rule:** Calculate your monthly international transfer count. If over 50 transfers/month, Scale plan (A$149) beats Wise's per-transaction fees. If under 30 transfers, Wise wins.
</Tip>

## AUSTRAC Compliance

Revolut is registered with AUSTRAC:

| Requirement | Status |
|-------------|--------|
| AUSTRAC Registration | Registered remittance dealer |
| AML/CTF Compliance | Fully compliant |
| Fund Safeguarding | Yes (not ADI) |
| Identity Verification | 100 point check |

<Warning>
**Not an ADI:** Like Wise, Revolut is not an Authorised Deposit-taking Institution. Your funds are safeguarded but not covered by the Government Guarantee. For large AUD balances, use an ADI.
</Warning>
