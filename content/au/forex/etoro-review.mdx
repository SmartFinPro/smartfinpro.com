## Copy Trading Explained

### How CopyTrader Works

CopyTrader is eToro's flagship feature, allowing you to automatically replicate the trades of experienced traders:

1. **Browse Popular Investors** — Filter by performance, risk score, market focus, and region
2. **Allocate Capital** — Set a minimum of US$200 per copied trader
3. **Automatic Replication** — Every trade they open/close is proportionally mirrored in your account
4. **Risk Management** — Set maximum loss (Copy Stop Loss) per copied trader
5. **Full Transparency** — View complete trading history, stats, and risk metrics before copying

### CopyTrader Statistics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Popular Investors available** | 3,000+ |
| **Minimum allocation per trader** | US$200 |
| **Maximum traders to copy** | 100 |
| **Copy Stop Loss** | Customisable (25%-95% of allocated funds) |
| **Partial copy** | Yes (proportional sizing) |
| **Fee to copy** | Free (included in spreads) |

### Selecting Popular Investors — What to Look For

| Criteria | What to Check |
|----------|--------------|
| **Track Record** | Minimum 12 months of verified performance |
| **Risk Score** | 1-10 scale (target 3-6 for moderate risk) |
| **Max Drawdown** | Historical maximum account decline (target under 20%) |
| **Consistency** | Monthly return distribution (avoid extreme swings) |
| **Copiers Count** | Number of people copying (social proof, but not guarantee) |
| **Asset Focus** | Confirm they trade forex, not just stocks or crypto |
| **Country** | Consider Australian-based traders for AUD-focused strategies |


### Smart Portfolios

Beyond individual CopyTrader, eToro offers Smart Portfolios — curated, diversified portfolios managed by eToro's investment team:

- **CurrencyTrader** — A portfolio focused on forex-related strategies
- **Minimum investment** — US$500
- **Automatic rebalancing** — Portfolio adjusted periodically
- **Transparent composition** — Full visibility of holdings and strategy


<Warning>
**Past performance is not a guarantee of future results.** CopyTrader makes it easy to follow successful traders, but markets change and strategies can fail. Never allocate more than 20% of your total capital to a single copied trader, and always set a Copy Stop Loss. Monitor your copied traders monthly and remove underperformers.
</Warning>
<Tip>
**For complete beginners:** Start with a A$100K virtual demo account to practise CopyTrader. Copy 3-5 traders with different strategies and risk profiles. Monitor performance for at least one month before committing real funds.
