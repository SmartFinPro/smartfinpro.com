## Key Features for Australian Users

### 1. Brand Voice for Australian English

The brand voice feature is essential for Australian content:

- Train on Australian spelling (organisation, colour, behaviour)
- Include Australian terminology (superannuation, SMSF, ABN, TFN, ATO, PAYG)
- Set professional Australian finance tone
- Maintain consistency across team members


### 2. Superannuation Content Expertise

Jasper performs well with super content after proper training:

- **SMSF education:** Compliance obligations, investment rules
- **Contribution strategies:** Concessional caps, bring-forward rules
- **Pension phase:** Minimum drawdowns, transfer balance cap
- **Division 293:** Additional tax for high-income earners


### 3. Tax Season Efficiency

Maximise productivity during busy season:

- Individual tax return checklists
- Work from home deduction guides
- Small business instant asset write-off content
- CGT discount and main residence exemption explainers

### 4. Team Collaboration

For accounting practices and advisory firms:

- Role-based access control
- Shared brand voice settings
- Project folders for different clients
- Approval workflows for compliance review

</Tip>

## Privacy Act & ASIC Compliance Considerations


### Data Privacy (Privacy Act 1988)

Jasper's approach to Australian privacy:

| Requirement | Jasper's Compliance |
|-------------|---------------------|
| SOC 2 Type II | Certified |
| Data encryption | AES-256 |
| Data residency | US servers (permitted under APPs) |
| Data retention | Configurable |
| Deletion requests | Supported |

**Best Practice:** Never input client TFNs, sensitive financial data, or personal information. Use AI for templates and drafts, not client-specific documents.

### ASIC Regulation (AFSL Holders)

When creating content that might be regulated:

**Safe to use AI for:**
- General financial literacy content
- Market commentary (non-specific)
- Process explanations
- Blog posts and newsletters

**Requires compliance review:**
- Personal advice or recommendations
- Product comparisons with advice elements
- Performance projections or returns
- Financial Services Guide content
