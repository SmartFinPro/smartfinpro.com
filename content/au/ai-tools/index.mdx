### ASIC Compliance and AI Content

<Warning>
**Regulatory Notice:** AI-generated content for Australian financial services must comply with ASIC regulations, including RG 234 (Advertising) and the Corporations Act 2001. If you hold an AFSL, all AI output requires compliance review.
</Warning>

**What AI can help with:**
- General educational blog content
- Newsletter drafts
- Social media posts
- Client email templates
- Marketing copy (with compliance review)

**What still needs human oversight:**
- Personal financial advice
- Product recommendations
- Performance claims and projections
- Financial Services Guide updates
- Anything requiring AFSL authorisation

### Australian English and Terminology

Most AI tools default to American English. For Australian finance content, you'll need to:

1. **Train brand voice** for Australian spelling (organisation, colour, centre)
2. **Adjust tone** for professional Australian finance audience
3. **Include Australian-specific terminology** (superannuation, SMSF, ATO, PAYG, FBT)
4. **Understand local context** (financial year July-June, GST, negative gearing)

Jasper AI handles this well with its brand voice feature.
