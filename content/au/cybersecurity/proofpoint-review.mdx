## Key Features for Australian Finance Teams

### 1. Targeted Attack Protection (TAP)

Proofpoint's TAP analyses every email attachment and URL before delivery:

| Feature | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| Attachment sandboxing | Multi-stage analysis in isolated environment |
| URL analysis | Real-time and click-time scanning |
| Zero-day detection | Behavioural and heuristic analysis |
| Threat intelligence | Global threat network across 2.8 billion emails/day |

<Info>
**How TAP works:** Every suspicious attachment is detonated in a sandboxed environment that mimics real user behaviour, including clicking, scrolling, and waiting. This catches time-delayed malware that evades basic sandboxing.
</Info>

### 2. Business Email Compromise (BEC) Protection

BEC attacks are the most costly email threat for Australian financial services:

- **Impostor detection:** Identifies display name spoofing, domain lookalikes, and compromised accounts
- **Supplier risk analysis:** Monitors vendor email domains for compromise indicators
- **Payment fraud alerts:** Flags emails requesting financial transfers or payment changes
- **Machine learning models:** Trained on billions of emails to detect subtle anomalies
- **DMARC/DKIM/SPF enforcement:** Full email authentication management
