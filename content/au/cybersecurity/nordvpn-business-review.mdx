### Kill Switch

The kill switch prevents data leaks if VPN connection drops:

- **App-level:** Closes specified applications if VPN disconnects
- **System-level:** Blocks all internet traffic until VPN reconnects

In our Australian testing, the kill switch activated within **50ms** of connection loss — fast enough to prevent any data exposure on local and international connections.

### No-Logs Policy

NordVPN maintains a strict no-logs policy, independently audited. They do not log browsing history, traffic destinations, data content, DNS queries, or IP addresses. Only account credentials and billing information are retained for service management.
