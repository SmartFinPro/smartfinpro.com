## Neat Variable Home Loan Explained

The "Neat" branding reflects ubank's philosophy: **one simple product, no hidden fees, no package tiers**. Unlike CommBank, which offers five or more home loan products across multiple packages, ubank gives you a single variable home loan and lets the pricing speak for itself.

### What Makes Neat Different

1. **Single product, zero complexity:** One variable home loan eliminates the "which package?" confusion that plagues big four bank comparisons.

2. **Rate equals comparison rate:** Because there are no fees, the advertised rate and the ASIC-mandated comparison rate are identical. This is rare among Australian lenders and makes genuine cost comparison straightforward.

3. **No annual fee saves A$11,850 over 30 years:** Avoiding a typical A$395 annual package fee saves you A$11,850 — money that compounds when redirected into extra repayments.

4. **NAB infrastructure:** ubank uses NAB's lending systems, credit assessment, and regulatory framework. You get fintech pricing with big four operational depth.

5. **Redraw as offset alternative:** While there is no offset account, the unlimited free redraw facility provides a similar interest-saving effect. Extra repayments reduce your loan balance and therefore your interest charges immediately.

<Warning>
**Redraw is not offset.** With an offset account, your money sits in a separate transaction account and reduces the loan balance for interest calculation only. With redraw, extra repayments go directly onto the loan balance, and accessing those funds requires a request (typically processed within 24 hours). For most borrowers making occasional extra repayments, this distinction is minor. But if you regularly park large sums and need instant daily access, an offset account at ANZ Simplicity PLUS may suit you better.
</Warning>

<Info>
**Why does NAB backing matter?** Unlike standalone fintech lenders, ubank operates as a division of an APRA-regulated ADI. This means NAB's balance sheet, capital reserves, and prudential oversight stand behind every ubank home loan. In a credit crisis, this institutional backing provides a level of stability that non-ADI lenders cannot match. Athena, by comparison, is funded by institutional investors but does not hold ADI status.
</Info>

## ubank Home Loan Application Process

Applying for a ubank Neat Variable Home Loan is a fully digital process. There are no branch appointments, paper forms, or fax requirements — everything happens through ubank's online portal and the NAB-backed mobile app. Here is what to expect at each stage.

**Step 1 — Online application (approx. 15 minutes).** You complete the application at ubank.com.au using the online eligibility calculator, which estimates your borrowing capacity based on income, expenses, existing debts, and deposit amount. The application itself covers personal details, employment, income, assets, liabilities, and the property you want to purchase or refinance. Document upload is integrated into the form — payslips, bank statements, and identity documents are uploaded directly without needing to send physical copies.

**Step 2 — Serviceability assessment.** ubank uses NAB's credit assessment engine to verify your income, run responsible lending checks under the National Consumer Credit Protection Act 2009, and calculate your serviceable borrowing capacity. The serviceability calculator on ubank's website provides an accurate estimate before you apply. For most straightforward PAYG applications, this assessment completes automatically within hours.

**Step 3 — Conditional approval.** Our testing showed an average of 3 business days for conditional approval — the fastest among the big four and competitive with digital-first lenders. You receive written conditional approval outlining the loan amount, rate, and conditions to be met before full approval.

**Step 4 — Property valuation.** ubank arranges and pays for a property valuation through their panel valuers. This typically takes 3–5 business days and is required before unconditional approval. The valuation confirms the property value justifies the loan amount and LVR tier applied.

**Step 5 — Unconditional approval and settlement.** Full unconditional approval follows within 5–10 business days of a clean valuation. Settlement is coordinated digitally and typically completes within 21 business days of unconditional approval — standard for the industry. For refinancers, ubank manages the discharge request with your existing lender.

<Tip>
**Refinancers:** Start your ubank application at least 6–8 weeks before your existing fixed rate expires to allow time for valuation and settlement without gap. ubank's discharge management team handles the coordination with your current lender at no cost.
</Tip>
