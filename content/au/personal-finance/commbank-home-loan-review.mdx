- **Unlimited additional repayments** on variable loans with free redraw

### How the Offset Account Works

A 100% offset account is a transaction account linked to your home loan. The balance in your offset account is deducted from your outstanding loan balance before interest is calculated. If you owe A$600,000 and hold A$80,000 in your offset account, you only pay interest on A$520,000. Unlike extra repayments, which reduce the loan principal permanently (and may trigger redraw restrictions on fixed loans), money in an offset account remains fully accessible at all times. You can deposit and withdraw freely without affecting your loan structure.

This flexibility makes the offset account particularly valuable for borrowers who maintain a salary buffer, hold an emergency fund, or accumulate savings for renovations or investments. The interest savings compound over time — on a A$600,000 loan at 6.19%, maintaining A$80,000 in offset saves approximately A$4,952 per year in interest and reduces the total loan term by several years.

### Digital Experience

CommBank's digital home loan tools set the benchmark for Australian banking. The CommBank app allows borrowers to view real-time balances, make extra repayments in two taps, manage their offset account seamlessly, track estimated property values with integrated suburb data, receive digital pre-approval within 1-2 business days, and access categorised spending analytics to manage mortgage affordability. The app is consistently rated the highest among all Australian bank apps.

<Tip>
**Offset account strategy:** If you have A$50,000 or more in savings, a 100% offset account on a Wealth Package loan can save you significantly more than the A$395 annual fee. On a A$600,000 loan at 6.34%, A$50,000 in offset saves approximately A$3,170 per year in interest — more than eight times the annual fee.
</Tip>

## Our 6-Month Testing Results

We applied for and managed a CommBank Wealth Package Variable Home Loan over a 6-month period, testing every aspect of the borrower experience from initial application through ongoing loan management, rate review requests, and customer support across all channels.

### Application & Approval

Our online pre-approval was returned within 1 business day. A dedicated home lending specialist was assigned within 24 hours of submitting the full application, and proactive communication via SMS and in-app notifications kept us informed throughout the 7 business day approval period. Document upload through the app was excellent — we submitted payslips, bank statements, and identification directly from the phone. Settlement completed on the scheduled date without delays.

The full approval process took 7 business days for our standard PAYG application with a single borrower and single security property. CommBank's credit assessment team requested one round of additional documentation — three months of bank statements from a secondary account — which we uploaded directly through the app within minutes. Complex applications involving self-employment income, trust structures, or multiple security properties should expect 10-15 business days based on current processing volumes.

The CommBank app earned a 5 out of 5 in our testing. Real-time loan balance visibility, two-tap extra repayments, seamless offset account integration, a monthly-updated property value tracker, a clear and downloadable repayment schedule, and an in-app rate review request feature collectively made this the most complete home loan management app we have tested across all Australian lenders.

After 6 months of hands-on testing, CommBank earns **4.6 out of 5 stars** as one of Australia's strongest all-round home loan offerings. CommBank is not the cheapest home loan in Australia — online lenders like Athena offer rates 0.30% or more below CommBank's best variable rate, and ANZ provides an offset account without an annual package fee. The combination of Australia's best banking app, a nationwide branch network with dedicated specialists, a fully featured 100% offset account, rate lock for up to 90 days, and the reliability of the country's largest APRA-regulated bank makes CommBank the right choice for borrowers who value service, convenience, and institutional security alongside competitive pricing.

Over a 30-year loan term, the net saving compared to the standard variable rate exceeds A$27,000 after accounting for all Wealth Package fees.

<details>
<summary>Is CommBank regulated by APRA?</summary>

Yes. Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA) is one of Australia's big four banks and an Authorised Deposit-taking Institution prudentially regulated by APRA. <summary>What is CommBank's lowest home loan rate?</summary>
