## Automatic Rate Drop Explained

Athena's Automatic Rate Drop is their most distinctive feature. Unlike big banks where you need to proactively call and negotiate for rate reductions, Athena automatically lowers your rate as your loan-to-value ratio improves.

### How It Works

| LVR Threshold | What Happens | Typical Rate Reduction |
|---------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **LVR drops below 80%** | Rate automatically decreases | ~0.05% |
| **LVR drops below 70%** | Rate automatically decreases | ~0.05% |
| **LVR drops below 60%** | Rate automatically decreases | ~0.05% |

### Automatic Rate Drop vs Bank Negotiation

| Factor | Athena Automatic Rate Drop | Big Four Rate Negotiation |
|--------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Trigger** | Automatic — no action required | You must call and request |
| **Timing** | Immediate when threshold crossed | Only when you negotiate |
| **Certainty** | Guaranteed rate reduction | Outcome uncertain |
| **Frequency** | Every LVR threshold | Typically annual at best |
| **Effort required** | None | 30-60 min phone call |
| **Success rate** | 100% | Varies — often partial |

### Real-World Rate Drop Example

Consider a A$500,000 home loan on a property valued at A$625,000 (80% LVR):

| Year | Loan Balance | Property Value | LVR | Athena Rate |
|------|-------------|---------------|-----|-------------|
| **Year 1** | A$500,000 | A$625,000 | 80% | 5.99% |
| **Year 3** | A$472,000 | A$650,000 | 72.6% | 5.94% |
| **Year 6** | A$430,000 | A$690,000 | 62.3% | 5.89% |
| **Year 10** | A$370,000 | A$750,000 | 49.3% | 5.89% |

*Property values assume modest 1.5% annual growth. Loan balance assumes P&I repayments only.*

<Tip>
**Accelerate your rate drops:** Making extra repayments reduces your LVR faster, triggering Automatic Rate Drops sooner. Every additional dollar you repay brings you closer to the next rate threshold, creating a virtuous cycle of lower interest and faster paydown.
</Tip>
