## Offset Account Deep Dive

The offset account is ANZ Simplicity PLUS's defining feature. Here is a detailed analysis of how it works and the savings it generates.

### How ANZ Offset Works

The ANZ offset account functions as a standard transaction account (with a debit card, internet banking, and direct debit capabilities) that is linked to your home loan. Interest on your loan is calculated daily on the net balance after offset.

| Loan Balance | Offset Balance | Interest Charged On | Daily Interest Saving |
|-------------|---------------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| A$500,000 | A$0 | A$500,000 | A$0 |
| A$500,000 | A$20,000 | A$480,000 | A$3.45 |
| A$500,000 | A$50,000 | A$450,000 | A$8.62 |
| A$500,000 | A$100,000 | A$400,000 | A$17.23 |
| A$500,000 | A$150,000 | A$350,000 | A$25.85 |

*Based on 6.29% p.a. variable rate. Daily savings calculated as (offset balance x rate) / 365.*

### Lifetime Offset Savings (A$500,000 Loan, 6.29%, 30 Years)

| Offset Balance Maintained | Total Interest Saved | Loan Term Reduction |
|--------------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| **A$10,000** | A$39,400 | 1 year 2 months |
| **A$20,000** | A$74,200 | 2 years 3 months |
| **A$50,000** | A$164,800 | 5 years 1 month |
| **A$100,000** | A$281,200 | 8 years 9 months |
| **A$150,000** | A$362,400 | 11 years 4 months |

<Tip>
**Salary crediting strategy:** Have your salary paid directly into your offset account. Even though you spend from the account throughout the month, the daily interest calculation means every dollar sitting in offset — even temporarily — reduces your interest. This strategy maximises the time your money spends reducing your loan balance.
</Tip>

### Offset vs Redraw — Key Differences

| Feature | Offset Account | Redraw Facility |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Accessibility** | Instant via debit card | Transfer required |
| **Tax implications** | No tax event | May have tax implications for investors |
| **Interest impact** | Reduces interest daily | Reduces interest daily |
| **Transaction account** | Yes — full transaction capability | No — loan facility only |
| **Direct debits** | Yes | No |
| **Investment property** | Preserves tax deductibility | May reduce deductible debt |

<Warning>
**Important for investors:** If your home loan is for an investment property, using an offset account preserves the tax deductibility of your loan interest. Placing spare funds in a redraw facility and then withdrawing them for personal use could reduce the tax-deductible portion of your investment loan. Seek advice from a qualified tax professional.
</Warning>
