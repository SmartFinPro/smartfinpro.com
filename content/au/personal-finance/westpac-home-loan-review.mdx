## What is Westpac Flexi First Option?

Westpac is one of Australia's big four banks, established in 1817 as the country's first bank. The Flexi First Option Home Loan is Westpac's flagship variable rate mortgage product, designed to offer flexibility in repayment structures while providing access to comprehensive banking features.

Westpac's Flexi First Option offers:
- **Flexible variable rates** from 6.39% p.a. for owner-occupiers
- **Multiple repayment schedules** — weekly, fortnightly, or monthly
- **100% offset account** with Premier Advantage Package
- **Split loan facility** to combine fixed and variable rate portions
- **700+ branches** with dedicated home finance managers

### Premier Advantage Package (A$395/year)

The Premier Advantage Package provides the most competitive rates and full feature access:

- **Discounted variable rate** — 0.10-0.15% below the standard Flexi First Option
- **100% offset account** — reduce interest charged on your variable portion
- **Credit card annual fee waiver** — saving up to A$249 per year
- **Home and contents insurance discount** — up to 10% off premiums
- **No establishment fee** — saving A$600 in upfront costs
- **Unlimited extra repayments** on variable with free redraw
- **Fee-free splits** between fixed and variable portions

### Repayment Flexibility

Westpac offers multiple repayment options to match your cash flow:

- **Monthly repayments** — standard monthly Direct Debit
- **Fortnightly repayments** — 26 payments per year, effectively paying one extra month per year
- **Weekly repayments** — 52 payments per year for those paid weekly
- **Extra repayments** — unlimited on variable, up to A$30,000/year on fixed
- **Repayment holidays** — available after 12 months of consistent repayments (up to 3 months)

<Tip>
**Fortnightly repayment strategy:** Switching from monthly to fortnightly repayments on a A$500,000 loan at 6.39% can save approximately A$78,000 in interest and reduce your loan term by over 4 years. This is because you make the equivalent of 13 monthly payments per year instead of 12.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Fixed rate exit costs:** If you need to break a Westpac fixed rate loan before the end of the fixed period, you may incur significant break costs. ## Our Verdict

After 6 months of testing, Westpac earns **4.5 out of 5 stars** as a strong choice for Australian borrowers who value fixed rate competitiveness and repayment flexibility.

**Bottom line:** Westpac's Flexi First Option with the Premier Advantage Package delivers a well-rounded home loan experience. The bank's fixed rate offerings are consistently among the most competitive from the big four, and the split loan facility gives borrowers a practical way to balance rate certainty with variable rate flexibility. The refinancing cashback offers can meaningfully offset switching costs. However, variable rates lag behind online lenders, and the approval process is slower than digital-first competitors.

For borrowers prioritising fixed rate certainty, repayment flexibility, or refinancing incentives, Westpac is a compelling option. If your focus is purely on the lowest variable rate, look to online lenders like Athena instead.
