## Canadian Pricing (CAD)

Revolut offers three tiers for Canadian businesses:

| Plan | Monthly Fee | FX Fee | Int'l Transfers | Cards |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------------|-------|
| **Free** | C$0 | 1.0% | 5 free/month | 1 physical |
| **Grow** | C$35 | 0.4% | 50 free/month | 10 cards |
| **Scale** | C$135 | 0% | **Unlimited** | Unlimited |

### Break-Even Analysis: Revolut vs. Wise

| Monthly Volume | Best Choice | Why |
|----------------|-------------|-----|
| Under C$20,000 | **Wise** | Pay-per-transaction cheaper |
| C$20,000-100,000 | **Wise or Grow** | Similar costs |
| C$100,000-200,000 | **Revolut Grow** | 50 free transfers |
| Over C$200,000 | **Revolut Scale** | Unlimited free transfers |

<Tip>
**Decision Rule:** Calculate your monthly international transfer count. If over 50 transfers/month, Scale plan (C$135) beats Wise's per-transaction fees. If under 30 transfers, Wise wins.
</Tip>

## FINTRAC Compliance

Revolut is registered with FINTRAC as a Money Services Business:

| Requirement | Status |
|-------------|--------|
| FINTRAC Registration | MSB registered |
| AML Compliance | Fully compliant |
| Fund Safeguarding | Yes (not CDIC) |
| Identity Verification | Required |

<Warning>
**Not CDIC Insured:** Like Wise, Revolut is not a bank. Your funds are safeguarded but not covered by CDIC deposit insurance. For large CAD balances requiring insurance, use a CDIC member institution.
</Warning>
