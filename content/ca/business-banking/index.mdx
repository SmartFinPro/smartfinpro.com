### FINTRAC Compliance

<Warning>
**Regulatory Note:** All business accounts in Canada must comply with FINTRAC (Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre) anti-money laundering requirements. Both traditional banks and fintechs like Wise are registered Money Services Businesses (MSBs) in Canada.
</Warning>
