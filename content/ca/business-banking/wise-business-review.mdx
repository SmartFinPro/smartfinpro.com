## FINTRAC Compliance & Safety

### Regulatory Status

Wise is registered with FINTRAC as a Money Services Business (MSB):

| Requirement | Status |
|-------------|--------|
| FINTRAC Registration | MSB #M08905000 |
| AML Program | Fully compliant |
| Identity Verification | Required |
| Transaction Monitoring | Automated |

### Fund Protection

<Warning>
**Important Distinction:** Wise is not a bank and not CDIC-insured. However, your funds are "safeguarded"—held in segregated accounts at major banks, separate from Wise's operating funds. In the unlikely event of Wise's insolvency, your funds would be protected.
</Warning>

**For large CAD balances requiring CDIC insurance:** Keep those funds at a CDIC member (Big Five, EQ Bank). Use Wise for international payments.
