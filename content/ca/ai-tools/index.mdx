### OSC/CSA Compliance and AI Content

<Warning>
**Regulatory Notice:** AI-generated content for Canadian financial services must comply with OSC and CSA rules, including NI 81-102 and NI 31-103 requirements. AI output should be treated as a first draft requiring compliance review.
</Warning>

**What AI can help with:**
- Educational blog content
- Newsletter drafts
- Social media posts
- Client email templates
- Marketing copy (with review)

**What still needs human oversight:**
- Specific financial advice
- Product recommendations
- Performance claims
- Risk disclosures
- Bilingual compliance (French/English)

### Canadian English and Bilingual Support

Most AI tools default to American English. For Canadian finance content, you'll need to:

1. **Train brand voice** for Canadian spelling (colour, centre, cheque)
2. **Adjust tone** for professional Canadian finance audience
3. **Include Canadian-specific terminology** (RRSPs, TFSAs, CRA, etc.)
4. **Consider bilingual needs** for Quebec clients

Jasper AI handles this well with its brand voice feature.
