## PIPEDA & OSC Compliance Considerations


### Data Privacy (PIPEDA)

Jasper's approach to Canadian privacy:

| Requirement | Jasper's Compliance |
|-------------|---------------------|
| SOC 2 Type II | Certified |
| Data encryption | AES-256 |
| Data residency | US servers (permitted under PIPEDA) |
| Data retention | Configurable |
| Deletion requests | Supported |

**Best Practice:** Don't input client PII or confidential data. Use AI for templates and drafts, not client-specific documents.

### Securities Regulation (OSC/CSA)

When creating content that might be regulated:

**Safe to use AI for:**
- General educational content
- Market commentary (non-specific)
- Process explanations
- Blog posts and newsletters

**Requires careful human review:**
- Investment recommendations
- Performance projections
- Risk disclosures
- Compliance documentation
