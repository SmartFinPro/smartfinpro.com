### Client Fund Protection

CMC Markets Canada maintains several protections for client funds:

- **Fund segregation** — client money held separately from CMC corporate funds at Canadian banks
- **Negative balance protection** — you cannot lose more than your account balance
- **Capital adequacy** — subject to CIRO minimum capital requirements

<Warning>

## Canadian Tax Treatment

### Tax Reporting with CMC Markets Canada

CMC Markets Canada provides tax documentation to support your CRA filing:

- **T5008 slips** — issued for securities transactions as required by CIRO
- **Annual account statements** — detailed trade history in CAD
- **Realized P&L reports** — downloadable for your accountant
- **Transaction history export** — CSV format for tax software

### Capital Gains vs Business Income

| Factor | Capital Gains | Business Income |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Inclusion rate** | 50% of net gains taxable | 100% of net gains taxable |
| **Typical CMC trader** | Occasional trades, holding periods of days+ | Daily active trading, high frequency |
| **Exotic pair trading** | Generally capital if infrequent | May support business classification if systematic |
| **GSLO costs** | Part of adjusted cost base | Deductible as business expense |

### Tax Considerations for Exotic Pair Traders

If you trade CMC's extensive exotic pair selection:

- **Higher spreads on exotics** mean higher transaction costs — these reduce your taxable gains
- **Swap rates on exotics** can be significant — overnight financing costs are deductible
- **Currency conversion** — the CRA requires all gains to be reported in CAD using Bank of Canada exchange rates
- **Record keeping** — exotic pair trades may require additional documentation of the currency conversion methodology

<Warning>
**Exotic pair trading adds tax complexity.** Trading currencies like USD/TRY or EUR/HUF requires converting gains from these currencies to CAD for tax reporting. Use Bank of Canada exchange rates on the trade date. Consider tax preparation software or a CPA experienced with multi-currency trading.
</Warning>
