## PIPEDA & Canadian Compliance

### Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)

Proofpoint supports PIPEDA compliance through:

- **Canadian data residency:** Email processing within Canadian data centres
- **Data minimisation:** Only necessary data retained for threat analysis
- **Consent management:** Tools to manage email consent and preferences
- **Breach notification:** Automated incident detection supporting 72-hour breach notification requirements
- **Access and correction:** Capabilities to locate and manage personal information in email archives

### OSFI Compliance for Financial Institutions

For OSFI-regulated banks and insurers, Proofpoint aligns with:

| OSFI Guideline | Proofpoint Capability |
|----------------|----------------------|
| B-13 (Cyber Risk) | Advanced threat detection and incident response |
| B-10 (Outsourcing) | Canadian data residency and contractual controls |
| E-21 (Operational Risk) | Comprehensive audit logging and reporting |
| IFRS Reporting | Data integrity controls for financial communications |

### Provincial Privacy Laws

Proofpoint also supports compliance with:

- **Alberta PIPA:** Personal Information Protection Act requirements
- **Quebec Law 25:** Modernised privacy framework compliance
- **BC PIPA:** British Columbia privacy obligations
- **Ontario PHIPA:** Health-related data protection (for insurance firms)

<Info>
**Quebec Law 25 Update:** Since September 2024, Quebec's modernised privacy law requires privacy impact assessments and enhanced consent mechanisms. Proofpoint's DLP policies can be configured to meet these strengthened requirements.
</Info>

### Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL)

Proofpoint assists with CASL compliance:

- Email filtering helps identify unsolicited commercial messages
- Consent tracking capabilities for marketing communications
- Unsubscribe mechanism monitoring
- Audit trails for compliance documentation
