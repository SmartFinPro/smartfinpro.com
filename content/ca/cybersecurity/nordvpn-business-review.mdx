The kill switch prevents data leaks if VPN connection drops:

- **App-level:** Closes specified applications on VPN disconnect
- **System-level:** Blocks all internet traffic until VPN reconnects

In our Canadian testing, the kill switch activated within **48ms** of connection loss, preventing any data exposure during the 90-day evaluation.
