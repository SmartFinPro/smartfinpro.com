### Optimization Engine: Never Miss a Deduction

Wealthsimple Tax does not just file your return --- it actively **optimizes** it. The built-in engine scans your data and flags opportunities you might miss:

- **RRSP contribution room:** Reminds you of unused room and calculates the tax impact of contributing before the deadline
- **Medical expense threshold:** Automatically applies the 3% net-income threshold to maximize your medical expense credit
- **Charitable donations:** Identifies when bundling donations across years could increase your credit
- **Moving expenses:** Flags eligibility if you moved 40+ km closer to work or school
- **Spousal credit optimization:** Suggests splitting deductions for couples filing separately to reduce combined tax
- **Student loan interest:** Carries forward unused interest for up to 5 years

<Info>
The optimization engine runs in real-time as you enter data. You will see a running estimate of your refund (or balance owing) that updates with every change, so you can immediately see how each deduction affects your bottom line.
</Info>

### Maximum Refund Guarantee

Wealthsimple Tax guarantees you will receive the **maximum refund** you are legally entitled to. If the software misses a deduction and you receive a smaller refund than you should have, they will cover the difference.

This guarantee covers:

- All standard deductions and credits
- Provincial and territorial credits
- Carry-forward amounts from previous years
- Family-related credits (Canada Child Benefit optimization, spousal transfers)
